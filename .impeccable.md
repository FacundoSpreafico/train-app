## Design Context

### Users
Primary user is the project owner themselves. They use the app during personal training sessions to follow the routine and track daily progress without friction.

### Brand Personality
Easy, clean, and calm. The interface should feel supportive and focused, helping training flow smoothly without visual noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-mode first, with restrained but purposeful color. No fixed brand color constraints from the user; palette should prioritize clarity, hierarchy, and a calm athletic tone.

### Design Principles
1. Make progress status instantly legible with semantic color cues.
2. Keep the UI calm: use a tight palette and avoid decorative over-saturation.
3. Emphasize key actions and states while preserving readable contrast.
4. Use consistent color meaning across the app (same hue = same intent).
5. Prioritize quick in-workout scanning over ornamental styling.
